    • About

    My name is Sophia Tisoc, I am 11 years old, and skateboarding is my favorite pastime because it makes me feel free. When I board my face is it the wind and I feel like a horse gallopping in a meadow. It is also fun because you can learn many tricks such as the ollie, a kick-turn, and riding without pumping. Learn more about these moves in the "my favorite moves" section.

    My favorite moves

    My favorite moves are: 1. The ollie. This is one of the most common tricks where you jump with your skateboard 2. Kick turn. The kick turn is a very useful trick to do a 180 half way up a ramp. 3. Moving without pumping. This trick is a bit hard but the basic rundown of it is when you put your weight on the back of your board and then drop it to create momentum to move forward.
